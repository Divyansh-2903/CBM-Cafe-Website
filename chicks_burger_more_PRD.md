## 1. Executive Summary

Chick's Burger & More (CBM Cafe) is a fast-growing, aesthetics-forward café and fast food outlet located in Jhotwara, Jaipur. Established in 2025, the brand has built a strong local following with a 4.6/5 rating on JustDial across 25 reviews and an active Instagram presence (@chicksburger__more) with 340 followers and 36 posts.[cite:2] Despite strong offline and social media traction, the brand currently has **no dedicated website**, limiting its digital discoverability, direct customer engagement, and online ordering opportunities.[cite:2]

This PRD defines the requirements for a responsive, mobile-first website that elevates the CBM Cafe brand, drives foot traffic and online orders, and establishes a professional digital presence in the competitive Jhotwara food market.

### 2.3 Problem Statement

CBM Cafe currently lacks a web presence beyond third-party platforms (Swiggy, JustDial). This creates several business limitations:[cite:2]

- The brand is **invisible on Google Search** for queries like "burger cafe Jhotwara" or "best burger Jaipur"
- Customers have **no single destination** to view the full menu, discover promotions, or contact the cafe directly
- All online orders go through **Swiggy**, which charges a 20–30% commission per order, eating into margins
- The brand cannot run **Google or Meta ads** effectively without a landing page
- Competitors who have websites gain search ranking advantages over CBM Cafe

## 3. Project Goals

### 3.1 Business Goals

1. Establish a professional digital home for the CBM Cafe brand
2. Rank on Google for local search terms (Jhotwara burger cafe, Jaipur fast food, etc.)
3. Enable direct customer inquiries and WhatsApp orders, reducing Swiggy dependency
4. Showcase the menu, pricing, and promotions in a single, always-updated place
5. Build social proof through displaying reviews and ratings

### 3.2 Website Goals

1. Deliver a **fast, mobile-first** experience (target: LCP < 2s, fully mobile-optimized)
2. Reflect the **youthful, aesthetic, Hinglish-culture vibe** of CBM's Instagram identity
3. Provide **complete menu information** with prices, categories, and food imagery
4. Include **direct action paths**: WhatsApp CTA, Google Maps embed, Swiggy order link
5. Support **SEO discoverability** with local metadata, structured data, and optimized copy

## 7. Design Requirements

### 7.1 Brand Identity

The website must feel like a natural extension of CBM's Instagram identity — youthful, bold, and aesthetic. The tone is **warm, approachable, and modern**.

| Design Attribute | Direction |
|---|---|
| **Primary Color** | Deep Black / Charcoal (matching cafe dark vibes) |
| **Accent Colors** | Warm Red + Bright Yellow (classic food/burger palette) |
| **Typography** | Bold, modern display font for headings; clean sans-serif for body |
| **Photography Style** | Close-up food shots, golden lighting, crunchy textures |
| **Tone/Vibe** | Youthful, street-food energy, Hinglish-friendly copy |
| **Mode** | Light/Dark toggle or Dark-first based on brand vibe |

### 7.2 UI Components Required

- Sticky navigation bar with logo and anchor links
- Full-screen hero with background food image/video loop
- Menu cards with food category tabs (Burgers / Wraps / Drinks / Combos)
- Floating WhatsApp CTA button (persistent, bottom-right)
- Photo gallery / masonry grid
- Review/testimonial carousel
- Google Maps embed
- Animated scroll reveals for sections
- Mobile hamburger nav

| 390px | Mobile (iPhone 14 / standard Android) |

## 12. SEO Strategy

To ensure the website drives real traffic from day one, the following local SEO foundations will be implemented:

**Target Keywords:**
- "burger cafe jhotwara jaipur"
- "best burger in jhotwara"
- "cheap burger jaipur"
- "cafe near jhotwara"
- "cbm cafe jaipur"
- "cold coffee jhotwara ₹49"

**On-Page SEO:**
- Title tag: `Chick's Burger & More | Best Burger Cafe in Jhotwara, Jaipur`
- Meta description: `Visit CBM Cafe in Jhotwara for fresh burgers starting ₹49, cold coffee, wraps & cafe vibes. Open daily 11AM–11:30PM. Order on Swiggy or WhatsApp!`
- Schema.org `LocalBusiness` + `FoodEstablishment` markup
- Image alt text optimized with food + location keywords

**Google Business Integration:**
- Embed Google Maps
- Ensure Google Business Profile is linked to website URL (post-launch)
